Our Story

Fifty years.
One family.
Greene County.

Scott Jaynes opened the first showroom on East Andrew Johnson Highway in 1975 with a simple promise: install every floor like it was going into our own home.

Five decades later, that promise still defines us. Family owned. Mohawk Five-Star Retailer. Largest showroom in East Tennessee.
